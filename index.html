Welcome to Tamagotchi

Game Instructions

  1. You pet dies if Hunger (feed), Boredom (play), or Sleepiness (light) reaches 10.
  2. To feed the pet - click on the feed button
  3. To play with the pet - click on the play button
  4. To keep the pet awake - click on the light button
  5. The player wins if the time ends without the pet dying
